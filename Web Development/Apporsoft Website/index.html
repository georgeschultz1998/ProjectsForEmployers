About Our Founder

Anthony Schultz

I am Anthony Schultz, a passionate and innovative software engineer and the driving force behind AppOrSoft. With a robust academic foundation, including Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science from California State University East Bay and Graphic Information Technology from Arizona State University, complemented by Associate degrees in Enterprise Security and Business Programming from De Anza College, I bring a diverse skill set and deep understanding of technology to the forefront of app development. At AppOrSoft, my role extends beyond leading the team; it involves constantly exploring cutting-edge technologies and creative approaches to develop apps that are not only technologically advanced but also user-friendly and impactful. My vision for AppOrSoft is to revolutionize the digital landscape by creating applications that simplify and enrich everyday life, whether it's through advanced AI-driven tools or intuitive user interfaces. By blending technical prowess with an unwavering commitment to innovation and quality, I aim to position AppOrSoft as a beacon of excellence in the app development world.
